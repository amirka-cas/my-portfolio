About Me

I am a motivated Frontend Developer specializing in HTML and CSS, passionate about creating visually appealing and user-friendly websites. My journey in web development started with curiosity and quickly grew into a disciplined learning process where I practice daily, build real projects, and continuously improve my skills.

I focus on writing clean, organized code and designing layouts that adapt smoothly across different devices including desktops, tablets, and mobile phones. I apply UI design principles such as spacing, typography, alignment, color balance, and accessibility to ensure a professional user experience.

I have worked on multiple projects including business websites, landing pages, login interfaces, and a skills platform called XirfadHub. Each project helps me strengthen my understanding of layout systems, responsive design techniques, and modern web standards.

My long-term goal is to become a full-stack developer and deliver high-quality digital solutions for businesses and individuals. I am open to freelance projects, collaborations, and continuous learning opportunities.
